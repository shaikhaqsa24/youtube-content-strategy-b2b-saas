# YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Project Overview

This repository contains research on YouTube content strategies used by leading B2B SaaS marketing experts. The goal of this project is to understand how successful creators use educational content, SEO, storytelling, audience engagement, and content distribution to grow their businesses and build brand awareness.

---

## Research Objectives

* Study YouTube content strategies used by B2B SaaS experts.
* Analyze how creators attract and engage their audience.
* Learn best practices for content planning and distribution.
* Document key insights from industry experts.

## Key Learnings

* Educational content builds trust and authority.
* Consistent publishing improves audience growth.
* SEO helps videos reach the right audience.
* Storytelling increases viewer engagement.
* Content should focus on solving customer problems.
* Repurposing content across platforms increases reach.
* Understanding the target audience is essential for success.
* Analytics help improve future content performance.
