## Video 1

**Title:** How to Get More Views on YouTube

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/@AlexHormozi

### Summary
Alex explains that successful YouTube content focuses on solving a specific problem for a target audience. Strong titles and thumbnails increase click-through rates, while delivering value keeps viewers engaged.

### Key Takeaways
- Solve one clear problem per video.
- Create attention-grabbing titles and thumbnails.
- Keep viewers engaged with valuable content.
- Be consistent with publishing.
