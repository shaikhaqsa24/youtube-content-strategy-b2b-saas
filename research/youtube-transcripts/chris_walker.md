## Video 1

**Title:** How B2B Companies Should Create Content

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/@ChrisWalker171

### Summary
Chris explains that B2B content should educate buyers rather than directly sell products. High-quality educational content helps build trust and generates long-term demand.

### Key Takeaways
- Focus on educating your audience.
- Create content that solves customer problems.
- Build trust before promoting your product.
- Consistency is key for long-term growth.
